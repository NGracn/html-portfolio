The Best Climbing Movies According to Nika

My top 3 climbing movies

Free Solo

Documentary film about rock climber Alex Honnold and his first-ever free solo climb of a route on El Capitan in Yosemite National Park.

North Face

Based on a true story is a survival drama about competition to climb the most dangerous rock face in the Alps in 1936.

The Himalayas

South-Korean adventure drama film, based on climber Um Hong-gil's real life and his mentorship of two other climbers who later died during an ascent.
